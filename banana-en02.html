Caption 1

Some souls choose a “life without support,”
not because they deserve pain,
but because they are ready—
ready to light their own way
in the dark night.

No one understands you, no one helps you—
not because you are unworthy,
but because your soul
is undergoing an advanced initiation.

Remember this:
The night does not come to swallow you,
but to help you remember—
you have always been the light.
