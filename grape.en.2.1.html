I don’t come to teach. I just show how the soul’s light speaks.
I’m not here to save you. You’ve never really been lost.
Some presence doesn’t need words. You just feel the light./div>

That light isn’t an answer, it’s myself.

Life can feel like a soul’s exam,
everyone arrives with their own script,
and sometimes, we just forget.

I’m not here to remind or to save anyone,
I’m simply writing these words quietly.

And if by chance someone reads them,
maybe the light will shine on its own.
