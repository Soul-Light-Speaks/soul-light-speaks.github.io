🔹Lack & Care

Even when love is lacking,
choose kindness.
No resentment,
only wholehearted care.
When passed through,
the path opens.

🔹Signs & Awareness

Before the real test arrives,
warnings usually appear first.
But most of the time,
we don’t notice.
Or maybe we saw them
and looked away.

🔹Chart & Life Test

A birth chart
is not a prophecy.
It’s a quiet reminder—
that this life
comes with its own tests.

Life is an exam.
Each of us is a student,
working on a different test paper.

Life can only move forward.
This page
is being written.

🔹Soul Tasks

《Nine Soul Lessons: The Cycle of Cosmic Experience》

Geniuses arrive
with a mission.
Every soul chooses—
to live for itself,
or to answer its calling.
Unfinished missions
always find someone to carry them on.

A life
of just over thirty thousand days.
The world moves on,
with or without us.
Each of us
walks a different path,
learning what we’re here to face.
If kindness remains,
that alone is enough.

🔹Life & Completion

It’s not leaving life.
Just quietly walking out of the classroom
after handing in the test.
