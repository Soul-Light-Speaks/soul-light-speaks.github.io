Life can only move forward.
This page
is being written.

Each lifetime
is like a book.

No rereading,
no flipping back—
only the moment
you are writing now.

You can turn the page,
but never go back.

The book
may be remarkable,
or it may be ordinary;
it may be remembered by many,
or known only to yourself.

What matters is never the plot,
but whether—
we have sincerely completed
the lessons this life demands.

Once experienced,
if you can write an answer
with a clear conscience,
you have fulfilled
your duty for this lifetime.

And the extra credit
has always been one thing:
kindness,
and helping others
within your means.

Kindness
belongs to no era.
