A life
of just over thirty thousand days.
The world moves on,
with or without us.
Each of us
walks a different path,
learning what we’re here to face.
If kindness remains,
that alone is enough.

We live
just over thirty thousand days
in this world.

Some arrive as visionaries,
rewriting the systems
that shape our world.

Some move forward as builders,
quietly advancing
the edge of technology.

Others bend toward the earth,
nurturing life,
sustaining us all.

You do not need
to measure yourself
by how others define you—

whether you are striving,
or simply resting.

Every place you stand
is part of what your soul
is here to learn.

As long as kindness remains,
and you walk this path to the end—

this life
has not been lived in vain.
