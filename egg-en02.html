Caption 1

Not everyone who chooses euthanasia is trying to escape.
Some have simply been in pain for so long that they’ve forgotten why they came.
Some have already completed their soul’s lesson in this lifetime,
and simply choose to “exit” as a way to end their journey.

From the soul’s perspective, this is not “wrong.”
It’s merely a postponed assignment — to be continued in another form of learning.

It’s not losing. It’s being exhausted.
The soul will return, in another time and space,
to complete the unfinished exam with gentler hands.

This is not to encourage early departure.
It’s a reminder:
Some people are not “giving up,”
but have reached a breaking point where even remembering who they are becomes impossible.
They are not weak — they have simply reached their limit.
