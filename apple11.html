🔹Belonging & Place

Some doors stay closed.
Not because you tried too soon.
Sometimes—
you’ll see in another room
that the door was never yours to open.

🔹Future & Direction

Prophecies are never complete.
The universe keeps some hidden,
to spare us from the pain.
If we knew it all,
our awe for life
might fade.

Fortune-telling shows
the path you were meant to take.
Your thoughts and choices
create a different path.
It may seem off—
but you’ve already
changed your future.

🔹Turning Points & Change

Bad luck isn’t always bad.
Maybe your soul just saved you from something worse.
