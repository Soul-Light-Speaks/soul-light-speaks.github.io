Geniuses arrive
with a mission.
Every soul chooses—
to live for itself,
or to answer its calling.
Unfinished missions
always find someone to carry them on.

Geniuses often arrive
with a mission to fulfill.

Some come to lift the Earth’s energy and awareness.
Some come to remind us
that every soul has a choice—
to embrace its mission,
or simply live for itself.

The universe shapes our path
according to the choices we make.
Unfinished missions
may be entrusted to other souls.
