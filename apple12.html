🔹Seeing the Light

You are not alone.
There is always a light that will open the door for you.
And now,you have learned how to open the door with your own light.

🔹Understanding the Light

I don’t come to teach.
I just show how the
soul’s light speaks.

🔹Becoming the Light

Some people
never have children.
Yet through love
and quiet kindness,
they move the heavens.
And miracles
find their way.
Pregnancy
is not the end.
Only kindness

Doing good deeds without expecting anything in return,
often brings unexpected turns of beauty
into your life.
