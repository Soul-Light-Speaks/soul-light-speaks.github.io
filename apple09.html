This section explores the lessons and soul growth within relationships.

You thought it was love,
but it was your soul’s test.
Late marriage
is not missing love,
but meeting the right one
at the right maturity.
The stage lights up,
we meet in different roles,
yet only after the curtain falls,
we truly greet each other.
Sometimes lessons appear
within love,
sometimes only after loss.
Either way, your soul has evolved.
