Among People

Letting go
doesn’t mean
you don’t love.
It means
you’ve learned
to let life
unfold.

Life as a Test

It’s not by chance—
it’s a promise the soul has already made.

Beyond Life & Death

Death is not the end.
The light still stretches ahead.
In a single thought, you find peace.

Becoming the Light

Chant with sincerity,
lost souls find their way.
Do good without seeking reward,
you uplift others and yourself.
Make vows with compassion,
your light reaches all beings.
